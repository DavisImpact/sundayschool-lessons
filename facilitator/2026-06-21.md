  <!-- Section 1 -->
  <div class="mb-12">
    <h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold mb-2">1. David and Goliath — The Battle Is the Lord’s</h2>
    <p class="text-sm text-slate-500 mb-4">1 Samuel 17</p>

    <div class="bg-white border border-slate-200 rounded-2xl p-7">
      <p class="font-semibold mb-3">Discussion Prompts</p>
      <ul class="space-y-2.5 text-[15.5px]">
        <li>• What stands out about David’s confidence compared to the army’s fear?</li>
        <li>• How does remembering past deliverances (lion &amp; bear) help us face current “giants”?</li>
        <li>• What does this story teach us about where real strength comes from?</li>
      </ul>

      <div class="mt-6">
        <p class="font-semibold mb-2">Application Question</p>
        <div class="bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-400 p-5 rounded-xl">
          What “giant” are you facing right now? How might remembering God’s past help change how you approach it?
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- Section 2 -->
  <div class="mb-12">
    <h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold mb-2">2. Relationships &amp; Christlike Love</h2>
    <p class="text-sm text-slate-500 mb-4">1 Samuel 18</p>

    <div class="bg-white border border-slate-200 rounded-2xl p-7">
      <p class="font-semibold mb-3">Discussion Prompts</p>
      <ul class="space-y-2.5 text-[15.5px]">
        <li>• Contrast Saul’s jealousy with Jonathan’s loyal friendship.</li>
        <li>• How is Jonathan a type of Christ in this story?</li>
        <li>• How can we better rejoice when others are blessed?</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- Section 3 -->
  <div class="mb-12">
    <h2 class="text-2xl font-semibold mb-2">3. Mercy and Forgiveness</h2>
    <p class="text-sm text-slate-500 mb-4">1 Samuel 24–26</p>

    <div class="bg-white border border-slate-200 rounded-2xl p-7">
      <p class="font-semibold mb-3">Discussion Prompts</p>
      <ul class="space-y-2.5 text-[15.5px]">
        <li>• Why did David choose mercy when he had the chance for revenge?</li>
        <li>• How is Abigail a type of Christ?</li>
        <li>• When have you felt prompted to forgive instead of seeking revenge?</li>
      </ul>

      <div class="mt-6">
        <p class="font-semibold mb-2">Application Question</p>
        <div class="bg-yellow-50 border-l-4 border-yellow-400 p-5 rounded-xl">
          Is there a situation right now where the Lord is inviting you to lay down a vengeful heart?
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
